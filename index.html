Who We Are

Kerala’s Finest Chips, Brought to Your Door

CHIPSICO FOODS LLP is a passionate food startup born in the heart of Kerala — the land famous for its rich culinary traditions and premium banana cultivation.

We combine age-old frying techniques with contemporary hygiene and packaging standards to deliver a product that resonates with both tradition and modern consumer expectations.

Our Process

Quality in Every Crunch

From selecting raw bananas to sealing the final pouch, every step is held to the highest food safety standard.

1
Premium Banana Selection Ripe, premium-grade bananas sourced from verified local farmers in Kerala’s fertile regions.
2
Washing & Precision Slicing Thoroughly washed and uniformly sliced for consistent texture and even frying.
3
Temperature-Controlled Frying Deep-fried in quality palm oil at precise temperatures for crispness without greasiness.
4
Post-Fry Inspection Each batch visually inspected — broken or discoloured chips removed before packaging.
5
Moisture-Sealed Packaging Heat-sealed, moisture-resistant pouches with batch tracking and full traceability.
